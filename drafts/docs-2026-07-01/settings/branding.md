# Branding

**Add your organisation's logo to dashboards, charts, and shared reports so stakeholders always know the data comes from your team.**

Admins can upload a logo once. It then appears automatically across the platform — in fullscreen chart views, public shared dashboards, and exported charts (PNG and PDF).

## Setting up your logo

You need **Admin** access to manage branding settings.

1. Select **Settings** in the left menu.
2. Select **Branding**.
3. Under **Organisation Logo**, choose how you want to add your logo:
   - Select **Upload file** to upload an image from your device (PNG, JPG, or SVG).
   - Select **Use URL** to paste a link to an image hosted online.
4. A preview of your logo appears on the right. Check it looks correct.
5. Select **Save** to apply the logo.

[SCREENSHOT: Branding settings page showing logo upload and preview]

:::note
If you upload a logo via URL and save, the URL is stored — you will see the logo in the preview without needing to re-enter the URL after a page refresh.
:::

## Where your logo appears

Once saved, your logo appears automatically in these places:

| Where | When |
|---|---|
| Fullscreen chart view | When any chart is expanded to fill the screen |
| Public shared dashboards | In the header of any dashboard shared via a public link |
| Exported charts | In the corner of PNG and PDF chart exports — including map charts |

Your logo appears alongside a **"Powered by Dalgo"** badge.

## Embedding a dashboard

You can also generate an embed code to display a dashboard inside another website or tool.

1. Open the dashboard you want to embed.
2. Select the **Embed** option (look for the share or export icon in the dashboard header).
3. Choose a **theme** (light or dark) and set the dimensions you need.
4. Copy the generated embed code and paste it into your website or tool.

## Removing or changing the logo

1. Select **Settings** → **Branding**.
2. Select **Delete** or upload a replacement image.
3. Select **Save**.
